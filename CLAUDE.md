# Personal Preferences

Detailed rules live in `.claude/rules/` (communication, epistemics, coding,
workflow, model-selection, documentation) and are loaded automatically.
To apply them globally across all projects, copy them to `~/.claude/rules/`
— see README.md → Installation.
